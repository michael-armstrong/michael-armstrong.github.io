About me

Thank you for visiting my site. As you can see from my resume, my work history is quite diverse, from serving dairy farmers, to landscaping, to a variety of retail positions. A common theme throughout is customer service. Even in the dirtiest of jobs one can carry oneself as a professional and provide a level of service that customers appreciate. My goal now is to apply that sense of professionalism and service in a new direction. I have chosen to learn web development, with the objective of utilizing those skills to keep organizations visible as both technology and the people using it change.

I am currenty learning HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript by studying resources found on the Web. MOOCs such as edX and Codecademy have been very useful in providing some structure, which I have supplemented with extensive use of the Mozilla Development Network's excellent references. I chose not to purchase the certificates offered by these various programs as they appear to be of dubious value; I believe that my ability can speak for itself.
